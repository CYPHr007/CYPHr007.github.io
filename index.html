Trajectory

The move that rewired the system.

The story makes more sense as a sequence of inputs and outputs than as a motivational speech. A few environments changed the direction of everything.

Acceleration

Learning fast enough to ship

In less than a year I moved from no coding background to building products, training models, packaging desktop software, and working with AI systems under real constraints.

NOUS

From predictive maintenance to maintenance intelligence.

My first business started as PILAR and became NOUS. The question that started it stayed the same, if machines show signs before failure, why are those signs still missed, but the answer has grown. NOUS is no longer just a model that predicts. It is becoming the memory layer for a plant: every recommendation, every decision, and every outcome, recorded and built on any industrial machine.

Main build

Maintenance intelligence, not just predictive maintenance.

NOUS started from what I saw on the industrial side: signal before failure, missed every time. That part still holds. What changed is the scope. NOUS is machine-agnostic, it retrains on any equipment's real data, and it now carries the decisions a team makes, not just the warnings it gives. From reactive to structured is the short version of where this is going.
